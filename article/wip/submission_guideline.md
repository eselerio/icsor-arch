# Submission Guideline

This internal checklist governs the article and its supplementary material.

## Benchmark status

Do not remove draft notices or replace placeholder values until one complete, article-eligible nine-model run has passed every terminal assertion. Never infer missing values from partial runs, combine different run contracts, or describe an incomplete ranking as a finding.

## Scientific contract

All tables, figures, and narrative values must derive from one accepted run containing:

- 10,000 accepted in-distribution ASM2d-TSN steady states;
- 22 inputs: hydraulic retention time, aeration, and 20 influent components;
- 20 effluent-component targets in their fixed order;
- nested totals of 500, 1450, 2400, 3350, 4300, 5250, 6200, 7150, 8100, 9050, and 10,000;
- one seed-42 master ordering and persistent five-fold assignment;
- XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost, AdaBoost, Random Forest, support vector regression, k-nearest neighbors, partial least squares, and a multilayer perceptron;
- paired raw and projected predictions for every scored row;
- COD, TN, TP, and TSS calculated only after component prediction through the common composition map; and
- separate mild and severe OOD strata generated by mechanistic simulation.

The simulator evaluates all 28 process rates without an artificial rate ceiling or rate clipping. State and initialization bounds are not reaction-rate clipping.

## Leakage control and selection

- Use the persistent outer-fold assignment at every nested size.
- Fit every scaler only on its applicable training fold.
- Use the same four-fold inner assignment across models for a given outer partition.
- Complete 100 sequential seeded TPE trials without pruning or timeout.
- Freeze each outer-fold selection across all smaller nested sizes.
- Perform a separate 100-trial full-data search before the final OOD refit.
- Do not reveal OOD response columns until settings, preprocessing, and full-data refits are frozen.

## Metrics and fold summaries

For component (j), use the applicable training-set population standard deviation:

\[
e_{ij}=\frac{\hat c_{ij}-c_{out,ij}}{\sigma_j}.
\]

\[
\mathrm{nMSE}=\frac{1}{20n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{20}e_{ij}^2,
\qquad
\mathrm{nRMSE}=\sqrt{\mathrm{nMSE}},
\qquad
\mathrm{nMAE}=\frac{1}{20n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{20}|e_{ij}|.
\]

Use exactly `nMSE`, `nRMSE`, and `nMAE` for these aggregate quantities. Report all five fold values, their arithmetic mean, and their sample standard deviation (`ddof=1`). Describe them as fold-variability summaries, never confidence intervals or significance evidence.

## Physical diagnostics

Compute diagnostics in the 20-component space before deriving composite quantities:

- conservation residual (\lVert Ac_i-b_i\rVert_2);
- conservation-violation frequency at (10^{-10});
- non-negativity-violation frequency below (-10^{-10});
- standardized negative magnitude;
- standardized projection displacement;
- backtracking frequency and accepted interpolation factor; and
- paired change in nRMSE, projected minus raw.

Retain raw and projected observations on identical sample identifiers. Report distributions and maxima as well as means.

## Timing contract

Separate hyperparameter-search time, preprocessing and fitting, raw inference, projection, and end-to-end projected inference. For each fold, construct the deterministic 512-row timing workload, apply five warm-up calls, and record 30 repetitions. Use the median repetition latency per fold and summarize the five fold medians as mean plus sample SD. Record the processor, process memory, thread limits, package versions, and operating system.

## Required article assets

Static methods tables must match the accepted configuration: simulation domain, solver settings, invariant operator, data protocol, nine model families, search domains, scaling policy, metrics, and OOD intervals.

Result tables must be regenerated together from the accepted run:

- dataset acceptance and physical validation;
- full-size ID accuracy and timing;
- projection effects and physical diagnostics;
- mild and severe OOD accuracy and diagnostics; and
- model-specific selected hyperparameters.

Main and supplementary figures must use all nine models and all eleven nested sizes unless a non-outcome-based layout separates panels. Do not select models because their findings are visually favorable.

## Supplement requirements

The supplement must contain:

1. the benchmark and fold-summary contract;
2. fold-level nMSE, nRMSE, nMAE, macro-(R^2\), physical diagnostics, and timings;
3. per-component physical-unit and standardized metrics for all 20 targets;
4. COD, TN, TP, and TSS metrics;
5. mild and severe OOD acceptance, prediction, residual, and displacement results;
6. fold-specific and full-data hyperparameters;
7. timing repetitions and summaries;
8. scientific provenance and computational-environment fields; and
9. all-model learning, component, physical, and OOD distribution figures.

## Generation order

1. Validate the mechanistic and projection equations and tolerances.
2. Generate or resume the 10,000-state ID dataset.
3. Freeze the seed-42 nested ordering and folds.
4. Run all outer-partition model selections.
5. Evaluate all eleven nested sizes for every model.
6. Persist paired predictions, metrics, diagnostics, and timings.
7. Select and refit all nine methods on the full ID dataset.
8. Generate and score untouched mild and severe OOD states.
9. Verify the completed manifest and artifact hashes.
10. Populate the article and supplement exclusively from that bundle.
11. Recalculate every reported mean and sample SD from fold rows.
12. Remove every placeholder notice and build both PDFs from clean staged sources.

## Final scientific checks

- Accepted mechanistic states meet residual and non-negativity tolerances.
- The full-precision invariant operator satisfies its null-space check.
- Process rates were not clipped.
- No validation or OOD row entered training, preprocessing, or selection.
- Every model adapter returns finite predictions in the fixed 20-component order.
- Raw and projected outputs pair exactly by sample identifier.
- Every projected state satisfies conservation and non-negativity tolerances.
- Composite quantities were computed after component prediction.
- Mild and severe OOD results remain separate.
- Every displayed number resolves to one manifest-tracked source row.
- The manuscript contains no implementation provenance, internal path, or placeholder text.

## Submission package

Prepare a flat package containing only the manuscript and supplement sources, required class/style files, bibliography files actually used, and referenced final figures. Exclude internal guides, analytical data, development files, and build byproducts. Compile from a clean staged copy and visually inspect every page before submission.
